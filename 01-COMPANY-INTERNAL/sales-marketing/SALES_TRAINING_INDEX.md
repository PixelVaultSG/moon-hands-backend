## 🎯 Key Scenarios Covered

### ✅ Client Already Has an Agent
**Script:** "What happens when they're sick, on lunch, or after hours?"
**Pivot:** Position AI as supporting, not replacing
**Close:** 14-day trial alongside current setup

### ✅ Price Objection
**Script:** "How much is a new patient worth? One pays for 6 months."
**Counteroffers:**
- Starter at S$347
- 6-month at $447 (save $300)
- Annual at $4,970 (2 months free)
- Referral = month 2 free

### ✅ Open Negotiation
**Framework:** Never discount without getting something
**Options:** Trade commitment for price, add value instead
**Floor prices:** Starter S$347, Professional $447

### ✅ Fence-Sitters (50-50)
**Script:** "What specifically is holding you back?"
**Tactics:** Micro-commitment, assumed close, loss aversion
**Close:** "Let's start trial today, you'll have data in 14 days"

## 📞 Call Structure Template

### Opening (0:00-0:30)
> "Hi [Name], this is Alex from Pixel Vault. We help [industry] capture 100% of their inquiries - including after-hours calls and WhatsApp. Do you have 2 minutes?"

### Discovery (0:30-3:00)
1. "How do you handle calls after 6 PM?"
2. "How quickly do you respond to WhatsApp?"
3. "How many calls do you miss per week?"
4. "What's your current reception cost per month?"
5. "How many more patients could you handle?"

### Pitch (3:00-4:00)
> "Our AI answers calls and WhatsApp 24/7, books appointments automatically, and costs S$547/month - less than a part-time receptionist for 3 days. 14-day trial, no commitment."

### Close (4:00-6:00)
> "I'm confident this will work for you. What if we ran a 14-day trial? You'll see exactly how many appointments it books. Fair enough?"

### Next Steps (6:00-8:00)
- Get email for trial setup
- Schedule onboarding call
- Send confirmation immediately
- Add to follow-up sequence
