# Pixel Vault Sales Battle Card
## Quick Reference for Live Calls

---

## ⚡ OPENING (First 30 Seconds)

**The Hook:**
> "Hi [Name], this is Alex from Pixel Vault. We help dental clinics capture 100% of their inquiries - including the ones they're currently missing after hours. Do you have 2 minutes?"

**If they say yes:** Proceed to discovery
**If they say no:** "No problem. When's a better time - this afternoon or tomorrow morning?"

---

## 🔍 DISCOVERY (The 5 Magic Questions)

1. **"How do you currently handle calls after 6 PM?"**
   - *Listen for: Voicemail, answering service, missed calls*

2. **"How quickly do you respond to WhatsApp inquiries?"**
   - *Listen for: Hours, next day, don't check often*

3. **"Do you know roughly how many calls you miss per week?"**
   - *Listen for: "A lot", "No idea", "Too many"*

4. **"What's your current monthly cost for reception/answering service?"**
   - *Listen for: $2,000-4,000 (receptionist) or $500-800 (service)*

5. **"If you captured every inquiry, how many more appointments could you handle?"**
   - *Listen for: "We're already at capacity" (upsell opportunity) or "We could take more" (perfect fit)*

---

## 💰 THE PITCH (60 Seconds)

> "Here's what we do: Our AI answers your phone calls AND WhatsApp messages 24/7. It sounds completely natural, answers questions about your services and pricing, checks your calendar, and books appointments automatically.
>
> **The difference:** Your current solution takes messages. Our AI actually books appointments while the patient is hot.
>
> **The cost:** S$547/month for unlimited calls and WhatsApp messages. That's less than a part-time receptionist for 3 days.
>
> **The guarantee:** 14-day free trial. If you don't book at least 5 appointments, you pay nothing."

## 🛡️ OBJECTION CHEAT SHEET

| They Say | You Say |
|----------|---------|
| "Too expensive" | "Compared to what? A receptionist costs $3,000/month. We're S$547. One new patient pays for 6 months." |
| "Already have someone" | "Great! What happens when they're sick, on lunch, or after hours?" |
| "Let me think about it" | "Of course. What specifically are you thinking about?" |

| "Not interested" | "Fair enough. Can I ask - are you not interested in capturing more patients, or just not interested in AI?" |
| "Need to ask my partner" | "Makes sense. What questions do you think they'll have?" |
| "Maybe next quarter" | "I get timing. But how many inquiries will you miss between now and then?" |
| "AI sounds robotic" | "Used to think that too. Want to hear a sample call? Most people can't tell it's AI." |
| "Had a bad experience before" | "Sorry to hear that. What went wrong? [Listen] Here's how we're different..." |
| "We're too small" | "Actually, small clinics benefit MORE. You don't have a team to cover phones." |

## 🎯 CLOSING TRINITY

### Option 1: The Trial Close (Use 80% of the time)
> "I'm confident this will work for you. What if we ran a 14-day trial? You can see exactly how many appointments it books. If you don't love it, cancel. Fair enough?"

### Option 2: The Assumed Close (If they're warm)
> "Sounds like this makes sense. Let's get you set up. Do you prefer morning or afternoon for our onboarding call?"

### Option 3: The Question Close (If they're deciding)
> "Assuming we move forward, would you want to start with voice, WhatsApp, or both?"

## ⏱️ CALL STRUCTURE

| Phase | Time | Goal |
|-------|------|------|
| Opening | 0:00-0:30 | Get permission to continue |
| Discovery | 0:30-3:00 | Find pain, build rapport |
| Pitch | 3:00-4:00 | Present solution |
| Objections | 4:00-6:00 | Handle concerns |
| Close | 6:00-7:00 | Get commitment |
| Next Steps | 7:00-8:00 | Schedule follow-up |

**Target call length: 8-10 minutes**

## ✅ DAILY CHECKLIST

### Before Calls:

- [ ] Set call goal (appointments booked, not calls made)

### During Calls:

- [ ] Listen more than talk (40/60 rule)
- [ ] Take notes on pain points
- [ ] Offer trial, not just information

### After Calls:
- [ ] Update CRM immediately
- [ ] Send follow-up within 1 hour
- [ ] Schedule next touchpoint
- [ ] Reflect: What could I have done better?

---

## 🚫 RED FLAGS - WALK AWAY

Don't waste time on:
- ❌ Price-only shoppers ("Who's cheapest?")
- ❌ Rude or disrespectful prospects
- ❌ Unrealistic expectations ("Book me 100 patients this week")
- ❌ No decision power ("I need to ask my boss's boss")
- ❌ Bad fit (genuinely won't benefit from AI)

**The exit:** "You know what, I'm not sure we're the best fit. I'd rather be honest than waste your time."

## 📞 FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCES

### If They Said "YES":
- **Immediately:** Send confirmation + calendar invite
- **Within 1 hour:** Welcome email with prep checklist
- **Day before onboarding:** Reminder call/text
- **Day after onboarding:** Check-in call

### If They Said "NO":
- **Immediately:** Thank you email
- **Day 3:** Case study relevant to their industry
- **Day 7:** "Thought of you" value piece
- **Day 30:** Check-in: "How are things going?"
- **Day 90:** "Still thinking about you" with new feature

### If They Said "MAYBE":
- **Immediately:** Summary email with trial link
- **Day 1:** Video testimonial from similar clinic
- **Day 3:** "Questions?" check-in
- **Day 7:** Limited-time bonus offer
- **Day 14:** Final follow-up: "Last chance for trial"

## 🆘 EMERGENCY PHRASES

**If you get stuck:**
> "Let me pause there. Help me understand what I'm missing."

**If they're confused:**
> "I may not be explaining this well. Let me try a different way..."

**If they're angry:**
> "I hear your frustration. Let me see how I can help."

**If you don't know:**
> "Great question. I don't have that answer right now, but I'll find out and get back to you within the hour."
