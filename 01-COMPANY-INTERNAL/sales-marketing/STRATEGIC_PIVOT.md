## 🎯 Option A: Vertical Domination (RECOMMENDED)

### Pick ONE Niche & Go Deep

**Recommended Verticals for Singapore:**

| Vertical | Why It Works | Booking Value | No-Show Rate |
|----------|--------------|---------------|--------------|
| **🦷 Dental Clinics** | High no-shows, expensive appointments | $150-500 | 15-25% |
| **📚 Tuition Centres** | Very SG-specific, high demand | $200-800/mo | 10-15% |
| **🏥 Medical Aesthetics** | High-value, appointment-heavy | $300-2,000 | 20-30% |
| **💇 Beauty/Wellness** | High volume, repeat bookings | $80-300 | 15-20% |
| **🏠 Property Agents** | Lead qualification critical | $5,000-50k | N/A |

### My Recommendation: **Start with Dental Clinics**

**Why Dental?**
1. **High no-show rates** (15-25%) = clear pain point
2. **Expensive appointments** ($150-500) = high value per booking
3. **Structured booking** = predictable patterns
4. **Less competition** than beauty/wellness
5. **Clear ROI** = easy to sell

### Dental-Specific Features to Build

```
Pre-trained Flows:
├── "New Patient" → Collect insurance, medical history
├── "Cleaning" → 30-60 min slots
├── "Consultation" → 15-30 min slots  
├── "Emergency" → Same-day priority booking
└── "Orthodontics" → Longer consultations

Booking Logic:
├── Buffer time for sterilization
├── Block lunch hours
├── Emergency slots reserved
├── Recall reminders (6-month checkups)
└── Insurance verification

Language Support:
├── English (professional)
├── Mandarin (elderly patients)
└── Malay (community)
```
