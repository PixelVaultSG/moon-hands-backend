## 🎯 THE AESTHETIC CLINIC PITCH

### Understanding the Buyer

**The Decision Maker:**
- Usually the clinic owner or medical director
- Often a doctor who also does treatments
- Overwhelmed with patient care + business operations
- Wants to look professional but not corporate

**Their #1 Fear:** Looking "cheap" or "automated" to high-value patients

**Their #1 Desire:** Capturing every inquiry without being glued to their phone

## 📞 THE AESTHETIC CLINIC CALL SCRIPT

### Opening (The Instagram Hook)

> "Hi Dr. [Name], this is Alex from Pixel Vault. I was actually looking at your Instagram - the work you did on that Ultherapy case was beautiful. 
>
> I'm calling because we help aesthetic clinics like yours capture inquiries they're currently missing - especially those WhatsApp messages that come in at 10 PM while you're doing treatments. Do you have 2 minutes?"

**Why this works:**
- Shows you've done research
- Compliment their work (flattery)
- Specific pain point (after-hours WhatsApp)
- Low-commitment ask (2 minutes)

---

### Discovery (The 5 Questions)

**1. The WhatsApp Question**
> "How quickly does your team typically respond to WhatsApp inquiries right now?"

**Listen for:**
- "Within a few hours" → Pain!
- "Usually same day" → Pain!
- "We try to check regularly" → Pain!
- "Immediately" → Rare, but ask how they manage it

**2. The After-Hours Question**
> "What happens when someone messages about fillers at 9 PM while you're in the middle of a treatment?"

**Listen for:**
- "They wait until morning" → Lost patient
- "I try to check between patients" → Burnout
- "We have a receptionist" → Ask about after-hours

**3. The Lead Quality Question**
> "How much time does your staff spend qualifying leads - separating the $59 trial seekers from the $1,500 treatment patients?"

**Listen for:**
- Frustration about time waste → Perfect!
- "We don't really" → Even better opportunity

**4. The No-Show Question**
> "What's your no-show rate for consultations? Industry average is 20-25%."

**Listen for:**
- High numbers → Confirmation & reminders opportunity
- Low numbers → They're already doing something right

**5. The Competition Question**
> "The clinic down the road - are they capturing inquiries you're missing?"

**Listen for:**
- Defensiveness → They're feeling pressure
- Indifference → Need to educate on competitive threat

## ✅ AESTHETIC CLINIC QUALIFICATION CHECKLIST

### Good Fit If:
- [ ] Active on Instagram/Facebook
- [ ] Offers treatments $300+
- [ ] Gets 20+ inquiries/week
- [ ] Owner is also practitioner
- [ ] Opened within last 2 years
- [ ] Located in Orchard/CBD/Town
- [ ] Currently runs ads
- [ ] Has waitlist or busy schedule

### Bad Fit If:
- [ ] Only does basic facials ($50-100)
- [ ] No social media presence
- [ ] Owner not involved in operations
- [ ] Part of large chain with call center
- [ ] Focuses on package sales only
- [ ] Price-focused positioning
