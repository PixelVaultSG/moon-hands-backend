## FRAMEWORK: From Current Behavior to Genuine Differentiation

### STEP 1: Current Patient Behavior

**How patients currently book aesthetic appointments:**
1. See Instagram/Facebook ad or friend recommendation
2. DM clinic on Instagram OR WhatsApp OR call
3. Ask about treatment + price
4. Wait for human response (minutes to hours)
5. Negotiate date/time
6. Receive confirmation (or not)
7. Show up (or no-show)

**Friction points in current behavior:**
- Average response time: 2-6 hours (business hours only)
- Response rate after 8pm: ~15%
- No-show rate without reminders: 20-30%
- Staff time per booking: 10-15 minutes of back-and-forth

### STEP 3: Indirect Competitors

| Competitor | What They Do | Why Patients Use Them Instead |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------------------|
| **Instagram DM auto-reply** | Basic automated greeting | Free, already on Instagram where they found clinic |
| **Clinic's own website form** | Contact form → email | Clinic controls it, feels "official" |
| **Phone calls** | Direct human conversation | Older patients prefer voice, complex questions |
| **Walk-ins** | Show up without appointment | Spontaneous decision, can't book online |
| **Competitor clinic with faster response** | Better manual process | Patient chooses clinic based on responsiveness |

**Key insight:** Our biggest indirect competitor is NOT another software. It's the **patient choosing a different clinic because that clinic responds faster.** Speed = competitive advantage for the clinic itself.

---

### STEP 4: Real Enemy (Status Quo)

**What 60% of clinics currently do: Nothing special.**
- Personal WhatsApp on owner's phone
- Answer when they can
- Miss inquiries after hours
- No reminders = high no-shows
- No tracking = no data

**Why status quo persists:**
- "It's working fine" (until a competitor adopts AI)
- "Too expensive" (but they don't calculate no-show cost)
- "Patients prefer human touch" (for complex cases, yes; for booking, no)
- "Too complicated to set up" (our onboarding is 15 minutes)

**Our pitch against status quo:**
> "Your current system costs you 5-10 lost patients per month. At $100 average treatment, that's $500-1,000 lost revenue. Moon Hands costs $347 and prevents those losses."
