# Moon Hands — Demo Video Script
**"The Receptionist That Never Sleeps"**
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Format:** Split-screen: Patient WhatsApp (left) + Admin Dashboard (right)

---

## SCENE 1: The Problem (0-8 seconds)
**Visual:** A clinic at 10pm. Closed. Phone dark. But WhatsApp notifications keep coming in.
**Voiceover:** "It's 10pm. Your clinic is closed. But your patients are still messaging."

## SCENE 2: The Bot Responds (8-20 seconds)
**Visual:** WhatsApp chat opens. Messages appear:
- Patient: "Hi, what are your opening hours?"
- Bot (instant): "Hello! Welcome to Glow Aesthetics. We're open Mon-Fri 9am-6pm, Sat 10am-4pm. Would you like to check availability?"
- Patient: "How much is Botox?"
- Bot (instant): "Botox is $15 per unit. A typical treatment is 20-30 units. Would you like to book a consultation?"
**Voiceover:** "Moon Hands answers instantly. Every time. In your clinic's voice."

## SCENE 3: Real Booking (20-40 seconds)
**Visual:** Patient continues:
- Patient: "Can I book HydraFacial for Tuesday 2pm?"
- Bot: "Let me check... I have 2pm available! To confirm, may I have your name and phone number?"
- Patient: "Sarah Lim, +65 9123 4567"
- Bot: "Thank you Sarah! Booking confirmed: HydraFacial on Tuesday at 2pm. See you then!"
**Right side:** Telegram admin alert pops up: "New booking: Sarah Lim - HydraFacial, Tuesday 2pm"
**Voiceover:** "Real appointments. Real bookings. Synced to your calendar. You get notified instantly."

## SCENE 4: The Numbers (40-50 seconds)
**Visual:** Animated stats appear:
- "24/7 availability"
- "Answers in 2 seconds"
- "Handles 70% of inquiries without human help"
- "$347/month — less than 2 hours of staff time"
**Voiceover:** "Available 24/7. Responds in 2 seconds. Handles the repetitive work so your team focuses on what matters."

## SCENE 5: Call to Action (50-60 seconds)
**Visual:** Moon Hands logo + website. Text: "Book a free demo → moonhands.sg"
**Voiceover:** "Moon Hands. Your clinic's AI receptionist. Book your free demo today."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Music:** Soft, ambient, trustworthy (not corporate/elevator)
- **Colors:** Navy (#060E1A) + Gold (#D4AF37) — on-brand
- **Font:** Inter for text, Playfair Display for headlines
- **Pacing:** Quick cuts, messages appear with typing animation
- **Tone:** Professional but warm, like a premium service
