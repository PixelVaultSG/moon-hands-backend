## 6. DATA & PRIVACY

### 6.1 Data Processing

We process Patient Data as a data intermediary under the Singapore PDPA. We collect and process Patient Data solely for the purpose of providing the Service — responding to inquiries, booking appointments, and sending reminders.

### 6.2 Data Storage

All patient data is stored in Singapore (ap-southeast-1 region) on encrypted infrastructure. AI conversation processing occurs via OpenAI in the United States under a Data Processing Agreement with contractual safeguards comparable to PDPA standards. No patient identifiers (names, phone numbers) are sent to OpenAI — only conversation content with anonymised session IDs. Daily automated backups are retained for 7 days.

### 6.3 Data Security

We maintain a comprehensive security framework including:
- Row-level database security ensuring your data is isolated from all other clinics
- Encrypted data transmission (TLS 1.3) and encrypted data at rest (AES-256)
- Prompt injection detection and prevention on every message
- Rate limiting and abuse prevention (per-patient and per-clinic)
- Automated security monitoring, intrusion detection, and Telegram alerts
- Weekly automated security scans
- Data Protection Officer (DPO) appointed — contact: privacy@pixelvault.sg

You may request a summary of our security practices at any time.

### 6.4 Data Retention

Conversation data and appointment records are retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days after cancellation, unless you request earlier deletion. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted from our systems.

### 6.5 PDPA Compliance

You are responsible for:
- Obtaining appropriate consent from your patients for AI-mediated communications
- Ensuring your privacy policy informs patients that an AI system will handle their initial inquiries
- Notifying us within 24 hours of any data breach or suspected unauthorised access
- Complying with Singapore PDPA requirements for patient data protection
- Ensuring your clinic's privacy policy references Moon Hands as a data intermediary

### 6.6 AI Training

**Your patient conversations are never used to train AI models for other clinics.** Each clinic's AI responses are based solely on your configured Clinic Data. We do not use your conversation data to improve models for other clients. All AI processing is conducted via OpenAI's Business API under a Data Processing Agreement that prohibits use of API data for model training. Conversation data is processed with anonymised session IDs — patient names and phone numbers are never transmitted to OpenAI.
